Work-Life Balance

Over the past few months I have become more and more aware of my lack of a work-life balance. Being someone who works in I.T. and also has a interest in computers and technology on a personal level, the lines between 'work' and 'play' were beginning to get blurred.

To be honest, I think I'm burned out when it comes to working with computers in my free time. I have recently taken steps to simplify my homelab, and I sold my gaming PC since I wasn't using it enough to justify how much it cost.
